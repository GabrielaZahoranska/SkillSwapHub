About SkillSwap Hub

SkillSwap Hub is a small Django app built for class: people post skills they can teach, browse what others listed, and use the contact fields to reach someone. The idea is peer learning without a complicated marketplace.

I built it because I wanted a straightforward place to keep “what I teach” organized and to practice full CRUD, auth, and Postgres in one project.
